A little bit about us!

We are a small Irish based company, established in 2012, with four staff members. Four friends who are golf mad!
The main Driver behind the business idea was an experience that we had whilst on a golfing trip...and it was terrible.
Transfers late, hotel poor, courses poor...all the things that you need for a good golfing holiday!
Our mission is that nobody that uses our company will be putt in the same scenario, but quite the opposite!
We strive to ensure that all of our golfers have a superb, stress free trip...even if we have to take you there ourselves!
Don't take our word for it, please take some time to read some of our recent reviews from customers that used our services!
